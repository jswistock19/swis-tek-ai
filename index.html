About Us

Built From the Ground Up

Founded by Jonathan Swistock in Richmond, Kentucky — SWIS TEK AI was born from a vision to democratize artificial intelligence, technology, and business growth for every entrepreneur.

Our Mission

Democratizing AI, technology, and business growth — giving every entrepreneur access to the tools, strategies, and intelligence that were once reserved for Fortune 500 companies.

Our Journey

2020
SWISTOCK founded — first ventures in CBD and hemp industry operations in Kentucky.
